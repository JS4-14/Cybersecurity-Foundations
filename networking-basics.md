Network - 2 or more devices connected together to communicate or share information
  --> There are private and public (internet) networks 
Internet - made up of many small networks joined together

IPv4 addresses were short on public IP addresses hence IPv6 - Supports a much larger pool of addresses.

MAC Addresses can be exploited with spoofing where an adversary can pretend to identify as a certain MAC address - poorly configured security designs will be susceptible.

Ping is used to determine relability of network between devices or website and see if theres an existing connection.
